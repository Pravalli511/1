During our Centenary we want to lay the foundations to be able to offer more paid internships to students and early career researchers. This year (July to September 2021) we offered three placements but we are eager to expand our programme, especially as opportunities for paid experience are increasingly scarce.

Thanks to the generosity of our donors we are able to offer four paid internships for early career historians, to be held between June and September 2022. These donors are recognised as Centenary Donors.
